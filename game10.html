Week 10: Michigan @ Penn State

Date: November 11, 2023

Stadium: Beaver Stadium, State College, PA

Result: W 24-15

Pre-Game Storylines

Michigan entered the game ranked No. 2 nationally, aiming to maintain their undefeated record. Hours before kickoff, head coach Jim Harbaugh was suspended by the Big Ten for alleged sign-stealing violations, leaving offensive coordinator Sherrone Moore to serve as acting head coach.

Game Recap

The Wolverines relied heavily on their ground game, rushing 32 consecutive times in the second half and not attempting a pass during that period. Running back Blake Corum led the effort with 145 yards and two touchdowns. The defense limited Penn State to 15 points, with the Nittany Lions' offense struggling to find rhythm throughout the game.

Notable Performances

  • Blake Corum: The senior running back rushed for 145 yards and two touchdowns, anchoring Michigan's offense.
  • Donovan Edwards: The running back contributed with a 22-yard touchdown run, complementing Corum's performance.
  • Defense: The unit held Penn State to 15 points and forced a crucial fumble in the second half.

Post-Game Remarks

Acting head coach Sherrone Moore praised the team's resilience amid adversity, emphasizing the effectiveness of the ground attack and the defense's steadfast performance.
